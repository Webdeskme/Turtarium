ADHD affects attention, impulse control, and hyperactivity.

üîç What is it?

Anxiety disorders cause intense worry or fear that affects daily life.

üëÅÔ∏è How to recognize it:

  • Feeling overwhelmed
  • Frequent worry or panic

üîç What is it?

Depression causes sadness, hopelessness, or loss of interest in things you enjoy.

üëÅÔ∏è How to recognize it:

  • Feeling sad or numb
  • Not enjoying things you used to like
  • Trouble sleeping or eating

üìå Next steps:

  • Talk to a healthcare provider.
  • Try therapy, medication, or self-care plans.

üîç What is it?

Sleep disorders make it hard to fall asleep, stay asleep, or feel rested.

üëÅÔ∏è How to recognize it:

  • Trouble falling asleep
  • Waking up often
  • Feeling tired during the day

üìå Next steps:

  • Make a bedtime routine.

  • Talk to a sleep specialist.

üîç What is it?

GI issues include stomach pain, constipation, diarrhea, or food intolerance.

üëÅÔ∏è How to recognize it:

  • Frequent stomach aches
  • Changes in bowel movements
  • Food triggers discomfort

üìå Next steps:

  • Talk to your doctor.
  • Try a food diary.
  • Adjust your diet (fiber, avoiding triggers).

‚ö° Condition 6: Seizure Disorders / Epilepsy

üîç What is it?

Seizures are sudden changes in brain activity. Epilepsy means having repeated seizures.

üëÅÔ∏è How to recognize it:

  • Staring spells
  • Muscle spasms
  • Loss of awareness

üìå Next steps:

  • See a neurologist.
  • Get tests like an EEG.
  • Treatment often includes medication.

üëÇ Condition 7: Sensory Processing Disorder (SPD)

üîç What is it?

SPD means the brain has trouble handling information from the senses.

üëÅÔ∏è How to recognize it:

  • Overreacting to light, sound, or textures
  • Needing to touch or move constantly
  • Feeling overwhelmed in noisy places

üìå Next steps:

  • See an occupational therapist.
  • Use sensory tools (fidget toys, weighted blankets).
  • Try sensory diets or calming activities.

üîç What is it?

This affects learning, reasoning, and everyday life skills.

üëÅÔ∏è How to recognize it:

  • Learning new things is hard
  • Needs help with daily tasks
  • Struggles with problem-solving

üìå Next steps:

  • Get evaluated by a specialist.

üîç What is it?

üëÅÔ∏è How to recognize it:

üìå Next steps:

üîç What is it?

üëÅÔ∏è How to recognize it:

üìå Next steps:

üëÅÔ∏è How to recognize it:

üìå Next steps:

üîç What is it?

üëÅÔ∏è How to recognize it:

üìå Next steps:

üîç What is it?

üëÅÔ∏è How to recognize it:
